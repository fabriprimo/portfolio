Imagery
One of the peculiarities of the visual of Degen_ are the cuts that are applied to the images in the visual identity. The cutouts are free-plan, the important thing is to maintain an aesthetically homogeneous structure and in line with the image present inside. There are also purple glyphs (hyphens) around the image; also in this case the composition is free while maintaining a proper balance with the other elements present.
